What is it?

  • An Access Token is a unique key that references your specific GroupMe user account. This token allows you to access only the groups and direct messages of which you are a member.
  • It is important that you do not share your access token with anyone. That would allow them to interact with GroupMe on your behalf and read all of your private messages.

How do I get mine?

  • Navigate to dev.groupme.com.
  • Sign in with your existing GroupMe credentials and password.
  • Entertain yourself with GroupMe's developer documentation, it can take up to ten minutes for GroupMe to generate an access token for your account.
  • Once credentials are generated, there will be a link on the right side of the navbar labeled "Access Token". Click this to display your unique token.
