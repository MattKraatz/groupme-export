Welcome to GroupMe Memories!

With GroupMe Memories, you can retrieve entire conversation histories from GroupMe in order to customize shareable eBooks! Traverse your conversation page by page, navigate to a specific date in time using a procedurally generated Table of Contents, or download the entire conversation as a CSV.

Click on individual messages to add them to a separate, shareable Memories eBook, complete with statistics, popular messages, and personal highlights. Share this personalized eBook with your friends and relive your favorite GroupMe Memories together.

Getting Started

  1. Register for an account on GroupMe Memories
  2. Navigate to the Profile section and make sure your Developer Access Token is provided
  3. Navigate to the New section
  4. Select a group from the Select Group dropdown. This is a list of all groups that you have access to via your Developer Access Token
  5. After you've selected a group, click the Build Collection button to retrieve the conversation history and build out your eBooks
  6. Wait for a success message, and for the cover of your eBook to display
  7. Have fun!

Features

  • Traverse entire conversation histories by flipping the pages of an eBook
  • Navigate your conversation by date using the Table of Contents
  • Download the transcript as a CSV file
  • Customize the title, subtitle, and foreward section of your eBook
  • Save your personalized eBooks for easy retrieval
  • Create a shareable link for both eBooks that doesn't require a login
